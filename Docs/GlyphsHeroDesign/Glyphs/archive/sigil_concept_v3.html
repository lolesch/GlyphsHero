Weapon — Action Node
No outer frame. Iconic stylized inner symbol — the weapon is the identity.
Condition notch — a gap at top of symbol. Empty = fires on own timer. Filled by a Trigger = that trigger drives it.
The notch is the visual grammar of dual mode: one marking covers both states.

Modifiers — Amplifier + Converter
